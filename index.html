Where is Eleanor Rose? (2019-2020)

The Project

Where is Eleanor Rose? is a 2D top-down adventure game, that addresses the 5 stages of grief and takes place inside of the mind of a teenage schoolgirl.

My Work

As the only programmer I coded every piece of gameplay behaviour. An essential aspect in game development is to code developer friendly - I made sure that the artists, audio designer were able to drag their content into the unity inspector without ever having to deal with the source code.
